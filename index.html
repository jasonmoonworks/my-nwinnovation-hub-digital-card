Helping Founders Navigate the Ecosystem

The NW Innovation Hub exists to make it easier for founders to access the right resources at the right time without having to navigate Oregon's entrepreneurial ecosystem alone. Whether you're exploring an idea, launching a company, seeking capital, or preparing to scale, we're here to help.
